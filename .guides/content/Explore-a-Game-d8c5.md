## Cookie Collector

You have four windows open in Codio right now.  This guide, your graphics window (the tab just to the right of the guide), your code file, and a terminal (the tab in the same window as your code.)

Type **python3 explore.py** in the terminal to start the game. You'll need to click on the graphics window to see it run. Use the arrow keys to move the happy face.  To play again, you need to end of program (click the x, or press control-c) and then type **python3 explore.py** again.

**Play for a minute, then answer these questions:**

- What happens when you collect a cookie?
- What ends the game?
- Look at the code — can you find where the alien moves?
